Welcome to the LABIC Time Series Health Repository

This repository was created to support research on time series, their algorithms, and their applications in the health domain. It includes tasks such as time series classification (TSC) and time series extrinsic regression (TSER). All credits to those responsible for collecting and making the datasets available are included on their respective pages. Finally, two other repositories are recommended for TSC and TSER in general, which directly influenced the creation of this repository. When using the resources made available in this repository, please include the appropriate citation to the respective datasets used and to our repository.
